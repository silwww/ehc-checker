## Calibration notes — applicable to all AFI load types

### E15 · AFI I.5 consignee pattern

Standard: Arla Foods Ingredients Group P/S (Viby-J, Denmark) as I.5 on all standard AFI EHCs = confirmed standing practice — never flag. Exception on Irish and Northern Irish destinations: named consignee appears in I.5 (e.g. Gobia Ltd, Dairy Consumer Foods, J M W Farms) — correct on Irish/NI lanes. Arla Foods Ingredients Group P/S in I.5 on Irish/NI EHC = medium warning — OV to confirm with exporter.

### E62 · AFI/Arla commercial document reference — invoice PO mismatch

I.17 on AFI Taw Valley powder/curtainsider EHCs is a load-level dispatch reference generated by Gregory Distribution, matching the ORDER field on Gregory dispatch note and the EHC filename. AFI/Arla invoices carry separate internal Arla purchase order numbers that routinely differ. Mismatch = silent pass. Cross-verification satisfied by Gregory DN and filename match. Does NOT apply to AFI bulk tanker LPC loads — there the WPC product batch reference is used as I.17 and matches the DN directly.

## Calibrations for Load type 3

### E18 · AFI Variolac product description variants

DAIRY WHEY POWDER and WHEY PERMEATE POWDER in I.27/I.28 are both accepted variants on AFI Variolac whey permeate loads at Calais FRCQF1 and Esbjerg DKEBJ1. CN 0404100200 is definitive for whey permeate; CN 04041058 confirmed for WPC on AFI Taw Valley bulk tanker loads. Both silent pass.

### E27 · DSV tanker range DSV533031-DSV533040 — four seal requirement

Applies only to tankers DSV533031-DSV533040 inclusive — ten tankers fitted with three top vents requiring 4 seals. Other DSV-prefixed trailers: standard seal rules. When four seals correctly present and photos match: clean pass, no flag. Flag only if seal count wrong (fewer than four = hard error) or photos do not match.

### E53 · Bulk tanker EHC — Delivery Note mandatory

For all bulk tanker EHCs (I.27 Type of packaging = BULK TANKER), Delivery Note must be uploaded. Absent DN = medium warning — load must not depart until cross-checked against I.19 seal, vehicle ID, batch/weight.

### E54 · Bulk tanker — in-situ seal photo cross-check

All bulk tanker loads and DSV533031-533040 tankers: in-situ seal photos must be uploaded. Cross-check all seal numbers visible in photos against I.19 and DN. Any discrepancy = hard error. AFI Taw Valley tankers: top vent seal(s) photos required; rear seal photo not required — document pack placed behind rear seal at checking. Rear seal number must still appear in I.19 and DN.

### E55 · AFI tanker DN seal field zero-padding

AFI Delivery Note "Seal No sale" field omits leading zeros (e.g. records 546679 rather than 00546679). Compare significant digits only — zero-padding difference alone = no flag. Genuine mismatch between significant digits = hard error per E54.

### E64 · AFI LPC tanker to Denmark — M119 at I.12

M119 in I.12 Approval No on AFI LPC tanker EHCs to Denmark (Arla Foods Ingredients Group P/S Danmark Protein, Videbaek) is a standing requirement — hard error if absent. Its presence is correct and expected. No flag of any kind — not even a blue notice.
